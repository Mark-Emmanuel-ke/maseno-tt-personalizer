================================================================================
    MASENO UNIVERSITY TIMETABLE PERSONALIZER - USER GUIDE
================================================================================

Welcome! This guide explains how to use the Timetable Personalizer to create 
your own personalized schedule.

================================================================================
GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

1. OPEN THE APP
   - Visit the Maseno Timetable Personalizer website
   - The page loads automatically - no login required!

2. YOU WILL SEE THREE MAIN SECTIONS:
   - "Enter Units" (top left): Where you add your units
   - "Selected Units" (below): Your current unit list
   - Timetable Display (right side): Your personalized schedule

================================================================================
HOW TO USE - STEP BY STEP
================================================================================

STEP 1: ADD YOUR UNITS
───────────────────────
1. Look for the text field labeled "Unit Code..."
2. Type your FIRST unit code (e.g., "CSC 115" or "ENG 101")
3. Optionally, type a unit name in the "Unit name..." field
4. Click the "Add Unit" button (or press Enter)
5. Repeat for each unit you're taking

TIPS:
- Unit codes should match those in the master timetable
- Unit names are optional (just for your reference)

STEP 2: GENERATE YOUR TIMETABLE
────────────────────────────────
1. After adding all your units, click "Generate TT" button
2. Your personalized timetable appears on the right
3. The timetable shows:
   - Days of the week (Monday - Friday)
   - Class times for each of YOUR units
   - Venue/Hall information
   - Time slots for your selected courses

STEP 3: SAVE YOUR SELECTION
────────────────────────────
1. Click the "Save" button below your timetable
2. Your units and timetable are saved automatically
3. When you visit again, your selection will still be there

================================================================================
UNDERSTANDING YOUR TIMETABLE
================================================================================

TIMETABLE LAYOUT:
- FIRST COLUMN: Days and dates (shows the day of the week and date)
- OTHER COLUMNS: One column for each of your units
- ROWS: Different time slots throughout the day
- CELLS: Show unit code and venue (if you have class at that time)

EXAMPLE:
If you added units: CSC 115, ENG 101, MTH 201
Your timetable will have columns for each, showing when each unit meets.

COLOR CODING:
- Header row (unit names): Green background
- Even rows: Light blue background
- Text: Easy to read with good contrast

================================================================================
MANAGING YOUR UNITS
================================================================================

TO REMOVE A UNIT:
1. Find it in "Selected Units" list
2. Click the "remove"  button next to it
3. Click "Generate TT" again to update your timetable

TO CHANGE UNITS:
1. Remove unwanted units (see above)
2. Add new units
3. Click "Generate TT" to regenerate

TO START OVER:
1. Remove all units
2. The timetable will clear
3. Add your new units fresh

================================================================================
SAVING AND PERSISTENCE
================================================================================

AUTOMATIC SAVING:
- Your unit selections are saved automatically to your device
- No account needed - saved in your browser
- Works even if you close the browser and come back later

IF YOU CLEAR YOUR BROWSER DATA:
- Your saved units will be lost
- You'll need to add them again
- The default timetable is always available

USING ON MULTIPLE DEVICES:
- Each device has its own saved units
- Desktop saves are separate from mobile saves
- Add your units on each device you want to use

================================================================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Why doesn't my timetable show?
A: Make sure:
   - You've added at least one unit
   - You clicked "Generate TT" button
   - A default timetable has been uploaded by the admin

Q: Do I need internet to use this?
A: You need internet to first load the app, but once loaded, it works offline
   for viewing your timetable. Adding units requires the app to be loaded.

Q: What if I have the same unit at different times?
A: The app shows ALL instances of a unit
   - You should select the time slot that works best for you
   - Remove the unit and re-add it with a note about preferred time

Q: Can I print my timetable?
A: Yes! Use your browser's Print function:
   - Desktop: Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac)
   - Mobile: Look for Share → Print option
   - Your timetable will print nicely formatted

Q: My units aren't saving - what do I do?
A: Try these steps:
   1. Refresh the page (F5 or swipe down)
   2. Clear browser cache and try again
   3. Try a different browser
   4. Check if JavaScript is enabled in your browser

Q: The timetable looks different today - why?
A: The admin may have updated the master timetable
   - Just regenerate your timetable to see updates
   - Your unit selections remain the same

   - Save as PDF using Print function
